Imagine celebration fueled by generosity

We are convinced that the excitement, joy, beauty, and awe of celebration can drive involvement and meaningful connections to relevant social needs, thereby sparking desires to effect change. There are non-profits in Los Angeles doing extraordinary work solving problems and changing the quality of life locally and globally. We exist to highlight them for new generations and new circles of influence.

Our events and parties support non-profit's efforts by bringing them the awareness and funds they need to successfully execute their missions. We believe that one move - to donate your birthday, your skills, your space to a cause event - can bring life. Therefore, we make giving a celebration. Our celebrations are uniquely curated to provide exciting experiences and new context for giving back. All donations to A Move For Life fund the benefit celebrations that allow us to support many organizations.

Our Declaration of Conscience

A cause with momentum can become a movement. A movement into something powerful. We are the catalytic agents that mobilize people behind shared passions and missions, using their personal platform.

We are fueled by three core beliefs:

To celebrate is to deem something meaningful and worthy of recognition.

Those who give freely create a better world.

The way we live can be a gift to humanity, so we live fully and for the service of others.
