A Move for Life

Our Declaration of Conscience

A cause with momentum can become a movement. A movement into something powerful. We are the catalytic agents that mobilize people behind shared passions and missions, using their personal platform.

We are fueled by three core beliefs:

To celebrate is to deem something meaningful and worthy of recognition.

Those who give freely create a better world.

The way we live can be a gift to humanity, so we live fully and for the service of others.

We provide guided opportunities to invest in generosity

Image movements fueled by Celebration

We are convinced that the excitement, joy, beauty, and awe of celebration can drive involvement and meaningful connections to relevant social needs, thereby sparking desires to effect change. There are non-profits in Los Angeles doing extraordinary work solving problems and changing the quality of life locally and globally. We support their efforts by bringing them the awareness and money they need to successfully execute their missions. Our celebrations bring circles of influence together with a featured non-profit organization to promote awareness and generosity. We believe that one move - to donate a birthday or attend an event - can bring life. The non-profit organizations we partner with are making significant impact in the areas of arts, education, health, and human services. Donations to A Move For Life fund our benefit celebrations that allow us to support many organizations. In addition, we designate a portion of our funds toward causes and organizations that positively influence the lives of children.
